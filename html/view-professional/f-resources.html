FACULTY RESOURCES

The Faculty Resources page hosts a variety of FREE teaching tools for educators, such as case studies, case challenges, interactive teaching modules, and the Infection Control and Radiology syllabi. A clinical teaching series is offered for CE credit to help educators learn to conduct an effective student-centered learning environment. Educators can assign CE courses and case studies through the assignment feature to supplement curriculum with current content. Learn about fundraising opportunities for students.

CLINICAL TEACHING MODULES AVAILABLE

This five-part faculty development series will discuss and describe the fundamental principles of clinical teaching. Participants will learn about the various phases of clinical teaching with practical teaching information to conduct an effective student-centered learning environment.
INTERACTIVE MODULES

A Periodontal Exam: Explanation and Implementation of AAP classification guidelines

The learner will experience a virtual soft tissue exam including the parameters of the new periodontal classification guidelines. Next, there are 5 patient case studies where learner will apply the material while developing critical thinking and problem solving skills. Lastly, upon completion of the case studies a final knowledge quiz.

Toothpaste Module

What's in it for you and your patients? This course module will go into depth about the benefits of stabilized stannous fluoride and how to communicate benefits to your patients.

Electric Toothbrush Module

Patient compliance is key to improved self-care and oral health. In this interactive module learn how to make an impactful and evidence based recommendation using motivational interviewing techniques proven to improve oral health. And practice using what you've learned in the patient cases at the end.

DENTAL RADIOLOGY SYLLABUS
Syllabus: Dental Radiology
The information in this 12-module syllabus is intended (1) to meet elements of initial educational/training requirements for Dental Assistant Students related to dental radiography; \ (2) to provide a framework for an in-service training program in oral healthcare settings to meet annual educational/training requirements as mandated by federal, state, local and professional organizations; and (3) to serve as a resource for oral healthcare personnel wishing to review evidence-based information on specific topics related to dental radiography. A PDF is available for each module that may serve as a convenient resource.
INFECTION CONTROL SYLLABUS
Syllabus: Infection Prevention, Hazardous Waste Management, and Hazard Communication Compliance
The information in this 13-module syllabus is intended (1) to meet initial educational/training requirements for Dental Assistant Students as mandated by OSHA and other federal, state, local and professional organizations, (2) to provide a framework for an in-service training program in oral healthcare settings to meet annual educational/training requirements as mandated by OSHA and other federal, state, local and professional organizations, and (3) to serve as a resource for oral healthcare personnel wishing to review evidence-based information on specific topics related to infection prevention, hazardous waste management, and hazard communication compliance.
